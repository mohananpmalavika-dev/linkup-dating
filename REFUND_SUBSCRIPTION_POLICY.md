## 2. REFUND POLICY

### 2.1 Refund Eligibility

**We WILL issue a refund if:**
- Charged more than once due to system error
- Payment attempted but service not delivered
- Duplicate charge detected (same amount within 24 hours)
- Billing error by payment processor
- Refund requested within 48 hours of purchase
- Service not functioning properly after purchase

**We WILL NOT issue a refund if:**
- Subscription worked as intended for entire billing period
- Subscription cancelled after use (beyond 48-hour window)
- User changed their mind
- Matches didn't meet expectations
- User was suspended for violating Terms
- Refund requested after 48-hour window

### 2.2 Refund Process

**Step 1:** Go to Settings → Subscriptions → View Receipt  
**Step 2:** Click "Request Refund" (only available within 48 hours)  
**Step 3:** Select reason for refund  
**Step 4:** Submit request  
**Step 5:** Confirmation email sent  
**Step 6:** Refund processed within 5-7 business days

**For older refund requests:** Email support@linkupapp.com with:
- Receipt/Order ID
- Reason for refund
- Screenshot of issue

Response within 48 hours.

### 2.3 Refund Timeline
- Refund initiated: Immediately after approval
- Payment processor processes: 5-7 business days
- Money visible in account: 10-14 business days (varies by bank)
- Not our responsibility if bank delays
- Ask your bank if refund not received after 14 days

### 2.4 India-Specific Refund (Consumer Protection Act)

**As per Consumer Protection Act, 2019:**
- Consumer has right to refund of goods/services
- Dating app matches: Not guaranteed deliverable
- Service quality: Subjective and opinion-based
- Refund discretionary within 48 hours (goodwill)
- After 48 hours: No refund as service was consumed

## 6. PLAN CHANGES

### 6.1 Upgrade
Upgrading from Monthly to Quarterly/Yearly:
1. Go to Settings → Subscription → Change Plan
2. Select new plan
3. Pro-rata charge applied (if any)
4. New plan effective immediately
5. Billing cycle resets

**Example:** Upgrade from Monthly (₹99) to Yearly (₹999) on day 15
- Already paid ₹99 for 30 days
- Yearly plan costs ₹999
- You pay: ₹999 - (₹99 × 15/30) = ₹948 (pro-rata)

### 6.2 Downgrade
Downgrading from Quarterly/Yearly to Monthly:
1. Go to Settings → Subscription → Change Plan
2. Select Monthly
3. Credit issued (if any) applied to account
4. Downgrade effective on next renewal
5. No refund for unused time in current period

## 7. FAILED PAYMENTS

### 7.1 When Payment Fails
If your payment fails:
- You're notified via in-app alert
- Email sent with retry link
- 3 automatic retry attempts (Day 1, 3, 7)
- Access continues during retry period

### 7.2 After Failed Attempts
- If all 3 retries fail: Access downgraded to Free
- You lose Premium features
- Data is preserved
- Manual payment retry available anytime

### 7.3 Retry Payment
1. Settings → Subscription → Retry Payment
2. Update payment method
3. Process payment
4. Premium access restored immediately
