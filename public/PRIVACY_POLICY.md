## 6. DATA SECURITY

### 6.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption in Transit:** TLS 1.3 for all connections
- **Encryption at Rest:** AES-256 for sensitive data
- **Password Security:** bcrypt hashing with salt
- **API Security:** JWT tokens, rate limiting, input validation
- **Database Security:** PostgreSQL with backups, no hardcoded credentials
- **Access Control:** Role-based permissions, audit logs

### 6.2 Infrastructure Security
- **Servers:** AWS with security groups and VPC isolation
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and alerts
- **Backups:** Daily automated, encrypted, geo-redundant
- **SSL/TLS:** Latest certificates, auto-renewal
- **DDoS Protection:** Rate limiting, WAF rules
- **Penetration Testing:** Regular security audits

### 6.3 Organizational Security
- Staff trained on data privacy
- Background checks for data-accessing employees
- NDAs with all contractors
- Incident response plan
- Data minimization policies
- Privacy by design principles

### 6.4 Data Breach Notification
If a breach occurs:
- Users notified within 72 hours
- Details of breach disclosed
- Remediation steps explained
- Offered identity monitoring (if applicable)
- Reported to authorities as required

## 7. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

### 7.1 India Users (DPDPA 2023)

**Right to Access:**
- Get a copy of your personal data
- Export in machine-readable format (JSON)
- Available within 30 days
- Go to: Settings → Download My Data

**Right to Correction:**
- Correct inaccurate data
- Update outdated information
- Free of charge
- Reflected within 7 days

**Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):**
- Request deletion of your data
- Process: Settings → Delete Account → Confirm (30-day cooling off)
- Automatic deletion after 30 days
- Some data retained for legal/tax obligations
- Deletion takes up to 90 days to process

**Right to Data Portability:**
- Get your data in a common format (JSON/CSV)
- Transfer to another service
- Available within 30 days
- No fee

**Right to Restrict Processing:**
- Limit how we use your data
- Temporarily prevent processing
- Useful if disputing accuracy

**Right to Object:**
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Opt-out of analytics tracking
- Opt-out of location-based matching
- Immediate effect

**Right to Grievance Redressal:**
- File complaint with our Grievance Officer
- Response within 24 hours
- Resolution within 30 days
- Escalate to authorities if needed

### 7.2 EU Users (GDPR)
EU users have all rights above plus:
- Right to lodge complaint with DPA
- Right to legal remedies under GDPR
- Data Protection Authority: Your country's authority
- Contact us for GDPR-specific requests

### 7.3 How to Exercise Rights
1. Go to Settings → Privacy & Data
2. Select desired right (Access, Delete, Download, Opt-out)
3. Verify identity with OTP
4. Submit request
5. Receive response within specified timeframe
6. For complex requests, email: privacy@linkupapp.com
