6Your Privacy Rights

6.1 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your data (with exceptions)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Get your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Opt out of marketing or processing
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: Contact your local data protection authority

6.2 DPDPA Rights (India Residents)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure of data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to opt out of profiling
  • Right to grievance redressal

6.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale or sharing of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

6.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, email us at:

privacy@datinghub.app

Include:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Clear description of your request
  • Copy of ID verification (for privacy protection)

Response Time: We will respond within 30 days (45 days for complex requests).

6.5 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for:

  • Marketing emails - Unsubscribe link in email footer
  • Push notifications - Settings → Notifications → Turn off
  • Location tracking - Settings → Permissions → Disable Location
  • Analytics - Settings → Privacy → Disable Analytics

7Security & Data Protection

7.1 How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Hashing: Passwords hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Access Control: Limited employee access with role-based permissions
  • Multi-Factor Authentication: Required for admin accounts
  • Firewalls & Intrusion Detection: AWS security groups and WAF
  • Vulnerability Scanning: Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Data Backups: Daily backups with encryption
  • Incident Response: Dedicated security team for breach response

7.2 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours (GDPR requirement)
  • Provide details of the breach and affected data
  • Recommend steps to protect yourself
  • Notify regulatory authorities if required

7.3 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your password confidential
  • Not sharing your account credentials
  • Using secure WiFi/cellular networks
  • Reporting suspicious activity immediately
  • Logging out on shared devices
